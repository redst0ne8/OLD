Divisions are a fun way to engage with others in the CSC Community. There are 3 divisions:

  • The Red Cooperative (Defectors)
  • Chronos Security Operations (Security)
  • Chronos Employees AKA Employee Division (Site Teams)

Each division has its own purpose, events, and functions, which will all be covered within each respective page found under the "Specific Division Information" tab in the navigation bar.

One of the bigest aspects of divisions are Official Raids.

Official Raids are raids where all divisions participate. CE handles violations while TRC and CSO battle it out!
